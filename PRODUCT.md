# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Parents, family members, and caregivers who want to create a personalized storybook starring a child. They arrive at the storefront to browse available stories, preview them, then personalize with the child's name and photo before purchase.

## Product Purpose

StoryForge sells personalized AI-generated storybooks. The storefront surfaces available story templates, lets shoppers preview a sample in their chosen language, then guides them through personalization and Stripe checkout.

## Brand Personality

Chaleureux, rassurant, unique.

Voice is gentle, family-first, and inviting — like a trusted children's book publisher. No hard sell. The product should feel safe, magical, and made-for-you.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages with big metrics and aggressive CTAs.
- Cold, clinical ecommerce with tiny product cards and discount urgency.
- Overly playful or childish interfaces that feel cheap or noisy.
- Dark mode or neon accents that clash with warm children's-book imagery.

## Design Principles

1. **Stories first.** The book preview and cover imagery should dominate; UI recedes until the shopper needs it.
2. **Gentle guidance.** Every step feels obvious and low-pressure — parents are busy and trust must be earned quickly.
3. **Language is part of the product.** Shoppers can read and buy stories in the language that matters to their family.
4. **Warm restraint.** Use ample whitespace, soft rounded corners, and a warm neutral palette that lets story art breathe.
5. **Consistency across surfaces.** Storefront, preview, and personalize pages share the same header, type, and color system so the journey feels like one app.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Target WCAG 2.1 AA for contrast, focus states, and touch targets.
- Support reduced motion; avoid auto-playing motion or animations that cannot be paused.
- Keep copy simple and scannable; button labels say what happens next.
- Arabic (RTL) support should be considered in layout direction when language selection expands.
